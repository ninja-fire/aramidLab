Aramids Zero Bridge is a decentralized cross-chain bridge focused on Algorand, EVM-based chains (and soon WASM chains). It bridges assets with a secure, cost-effective and fast communication protocol.

Aramid Wallet is a fully decentralised and secure wallet solution built on Algorand. It is the first Algorand wallet where you can manage, swap and bridge your assets seamlessly, without having to switch through several Dapps. Discover the future of interoperability, directly in your browser.

Diatomix is a decentralized options trading platform based on Algorand. There you can stake, participate in pools and enjoy a full decentralized AMM connected to our option market to trade and edge your funds securely with an advanced trading dashboard.
